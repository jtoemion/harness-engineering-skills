# Bleacher Report Football - Navigation Recipes

## football-news
Navigate to the Bleacher Report Football homepage and extract article links.

1. [goto] https://bleacherreport.com/football
2. [wait] 3 seconds
3. [extract] article links

## article-access
Access a specific article by slug.

1. [goto] https://bleacherreport.com/football/{slug}
2. [wait] 2 seconds
3. [extract] article content
